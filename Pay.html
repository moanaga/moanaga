How to pay for my booking.?
View all Topics »

1. Search for a schedule then place a booking by clicking on the right side that says book a tickett like shown in the following image.

 2. Choose your seat number, remember that the seat which are already booked will appear green while the one which you just choose will appear red and  now select your boarding point then proceed to booking as shown below.

 3. Feel in your personal details then proceed with the next step as shown below.

4.  Choose your payments mode which is currently tigo pesa then accept by ticking the terms of privacy and privacy policy and then confirm booking as shown in below picture.

 5. Get  your tigo verification code in your mobile number which is valid for 3minutes and feel it in the page given like below and then click next.

 6. Feel in your tigo pesa pin to confirm payments to the tigo pesa window that will appear in your screen and then confirm.

7. There you go, now you'll get your tigopesa transaction confirmation message for your made payments. Just simple like that, from there you can now print your ticket and enjoy your safari.
